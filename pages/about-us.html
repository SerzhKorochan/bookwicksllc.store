About us

About 2BROWS

Our view is that brows are as unique as fingerprints, so our stylists work to create a look that is totally bespoke to each and every client.

It all began with our Base Course. For the last 5 years, thousands of stylists have trained with us. and joined our quest for bespoke brows for all. Our passion for brows helped us to transform the salon brow industry. But we didn’t stop there.

Helping you become a Proffesional

 

Our mission is to empower brow bosses everywhere. We’ve developed courses to ensure you have an eyebrow solution for every client. From eyebrow lamination to microblading, we’re here to help with every step of your brow journey.

We’ve developed our training to ensure it’s accessible and open to everyone. We’re passionate about turning your passion for brows into your paycheck, which is why we offer so much more than just eyebrow training courses. We help you establish a career and become a Proffesional.

Your journey doesn’t end when you leave one of our training academies. We offer continued support to encourage you to be the best you can be. Not only will you have a dedicated account manager at our Leeds HQ, you’ll also gain access to our private social media groups, keeping you in daily contact with trainers and a community of brow bosses just like you.
